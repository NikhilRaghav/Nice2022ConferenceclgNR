About NICE' 2022

The National Conference on Information & Communication Engineering organized by department of computer science and engineering has become a primary venue for research and industry practitioners to discuss and resolve problems faced in the fields.
NICE’2022 provides common platform for discussing the most recent developments, innovative ad research works and related issues.
